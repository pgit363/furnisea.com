About us

Welcome to Furnisea International, your one-stop destination for all your home and office furniture needs. With a commitment to excellence and a passion for innovation, we offer a comprehensive range of services tailored to meet your every requirement.
At Furnisea International, we specialize in manufacturing high-quality home and office furniture that combines functionality with style. Whether you're looking for sleek modern designs or timeless classics, our skilled craftsmen are dedicated to delivering furniture that not only meets but exceeds your expectations.
In addition to manufacturing, we also offer expert repair services to breathe new life into your existing furniture pieces. Our team of experienced technicians utilizes advanced techniques and quality materials to ensure that your furniture is restored to its original glory, saving you time and money.
Looking to transform your space? Our home interior consultation services are designed to help you create the perfect ambiance for your home. From layout planning to furniture selection, our interior designers will work closely with you to bring your vision to life, ensuring that every detail reflects your unique style and personality.
Color consultation is another service we provide to help you choose the perfect palette for your space. Whether you're going for a bold statement or a subtle elegance, our experts will guide you through the selection process to achieve the desired look and feel.
For contractors and businesses, we offer bulk furniture supply services, ensuring timely delivery of high-quality furniture solutions to meet your project requirements. With our efficient logistics and competitive pricing, you can trust Furnisea International to be your reliable partner in furnishing success.
Experience the difference with Furnisea International – where quality, creativity, and customer satisfaction come together seamlessly. Contact us today to discover how we can turn your furniture dreams into reality.
